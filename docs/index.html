How it works

A service on your system creates a tunnel from a local gateway to a gateway on the internet. The local gateway shares and protects your local LLM APIs and apps. Devices on the internet can reach your local LLMs and apps.

APIs for Client Apps

Connect smartphone apps and other clients to your local LLM APIs.

Web Apps

Launch web apps running in your home lab on the internet.

Share

Share local LLMs and apps with friends.

Secure

Protect your local network by not exposing it to the internet.
